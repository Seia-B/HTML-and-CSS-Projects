Why coffee is amazing

Coffee is valued for its rich antioxidant content and is associated with several health benefits, including improved alertness, better athletic performance, a lower risk of type 2 diabetes, and protection against certain diseases like Parkinson's, Alzheimer's, and liver disease. However, excessive caffeine intake can cause side effects like anxiety, and coffee may worsen symptoms for some people with gastrointestinal issues.

Legend says an Ethiopian goat herder named Kaldi discovered coffee after noticing his goats became energetic after eating berries from a specific tree. Fun fact: coffee was originally chewed as a stimulant in Ethiopia, not brewed into a drink and the first recorded brewed coffee appeared in Yemen in the 15th century.
