Red represents a wide range of emotions and concepts.Including; passion, love, desire, as well as danger, anger and war. It can also be linked to energy, power and excitement. It's meaning can highly differ based on cultural context.

Violet represents a wide range of concepts. Including; modesty, innocence, faithfulness, as well as luxury, creativity and ambition. The definition differ depending on wether it's the color or a flower. It also vary accross different cultures and religions.

Orange represents different concepts. Including; warmth, enthusiam, creativity, as well as being associated with autumn, halloween, health and energy. It is a combination of red's energy and yellow's cheerfulness. It some cultures it can represent danger, temptation or a spiritual connection.

Blue represents a range of concepts, most commonly calmness, stability and trust. It is often associated with the sky and the ocean. It can also symbolize sadness or depression, as in the expression "feeling blue". In china, it can represent immortality and in some religions, divine grace.
